AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that focuses on holistic healing. It emphasizes balancing the body, mind, and spirit through natural remedies, diet, and lifestyle practices tailored to individual needs.

YOGA &
NATUROPATHY

Yoga and Naturopathy are holistic practices focusing on natural healing. Yoga uses physical postures, breathing, and meditation for mental and physical well-being. Naturopathy promotes self-healing through natural therapies like diet, exercise, and herbal remedies

UNANI

Unani is a traditional system of medicine with roots in ancient Greece, later developed by Arab and Persian physicians. It focuses on balancing the body's four humors—blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile—through diet, herbal remedies, and lifestyle practices to maintain health and treat illnesses.

SIDDHA

Siddha is an ancient South Indian system of medicine that emphasizes using natural herbs, minerals, and lifestyle practices to promote health and treat diseases. Rooted in spiritual and alchemical traditions, it focuses on balancing the body's energies and achieving longevity.

HOMEOPHATHY

Homeopathy is a system of alternative medicine based on the principle of "like cures like." It involves using highly diluted natural substances to stimulate the body's self-healing mechanisms, with the belief that a substance causing symptoms in a healthy person can treat similar symptoms in a sick person.
